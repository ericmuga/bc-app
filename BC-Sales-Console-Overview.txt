1. WHAT IS IT?
--------------
The BC Sales Console is a web application that sits alongside Microsoft Business
Central (BC). Its primary purpose is to give warehouse and dispatch teams a fast,
reliable way to verify and confirm that the right goods have been picked and
dispatched against a Sales Order or Invoice — without needing access to BC itself.

It also serves as a real-time audit trail for every document that passes through
the warehouse, including who confirmed it and when.


2. WHO USES IT?
---------------
- Warehouse supervisors / dispatch staff  — scan and confirm orders & invoices
- Sales coordinators                       — monitor order and invoice status
- Finance / compliance team               — verify E-TIMS invoice numbers and
                                            KRA QR codes
- Management                              — view analysis and summaries


3. HOW IT FITS INTO THE BUSINESS PROCESS
-----------------------------------------

  Business Central (BC)
        |
        |  Sends order/invoice data automatically via webhook
        v
  BC Sales Console (this app)
        |
        |  Warehouse staff scan the document barcode or enter the number
        |  System shows the document details and line items
        |  Staff clicks "Confirm" to log that goods have been dispatched
        v
  Audit trail recorded — document marked Confirmed


Step-by-step:

  1. A Sales Order is created or updated in BC.
     BC immediately sends the order details to this app (webhook).
     The order appears in the Orders list with status "Open".

  2. A warehouse user opens the app, scans or types the Order No.
     The app shows all line items, quantities, customer details,
     salesperson, route, sector, and printing information.

  3. The user confirms the order. The system records who confirmed it
     and at what time. Status changes to "Confirmed".

  4. When BC posts the Sales Invoice (after E-TIMS signing by KRA),
     BC sends the invoice details to this app.
     The matching order is automatically converted into an Invoice record.
     The invoice carries the E-TIMS Invoice Number, KRA QR code URL,
     VAT breakdown per line, and company PIN details.

  5. A warehouse or finance user can confirm the invoice separately,
     creating a second audit event.

  6. If the same document is scanned again after confirmation, the system
     logs it as a COPY scan (flagged in amber) but does not allow a second
     confirmation — preventing duplicates.


4. KEY FEATURES
---------------

DOCUMENT SCANNING
  - Search by Order No. or Invoice No. (typed or barcode-scanned)
  - Shows full document header: customer, salesperson, route, sector,
    ship-to name, payment terms, external doc no., E-TIMS no., QR code
  - Shows all line items: item, description, qty, UOM, unit price,
    amount excl. VAT, amount incl. VAT, VAT %, posting group
  - COPY detection: alerts if a document was already confirmed

AUDIT TRAIL
  - Every action is logged: received from BC, confirmed by user, copy scan
  - Shows who did what and when (full datetime)
  - Printing information from BC (printed by, printing time) is shown at
    the top of the audit trail

INVOICE VERIFICATION
  - E-TIMS Invoice Number from KRA is displayed on every invoice
  - KRA QR code URL is stored and accessible
  - VAT amounts per line and totals are visible
  - Company KRA PIN and VAT registration number are stored on the invoice

LISTS & FILTERS
  - Orders list: filter by status, customer, salesperson, route, sector,
    posting group, date range; export to CSV
  - Invoices list: same filters; shows totals strip (count, qty, amount)
  - Both lists show live grand totals (qty, qty base, total amount)

ANALYSIS (Reports page)
  - Bar chart summaries grouped by: Salesperson, Route, Sector,
    Customer, Posting Group, Date
  - Shows document count, total qty (base), and total amount per group
  - Date range filter; export to CSV

MULTI-COMPANY SUPPORT
  - The app supports multiple BC companies simultaneously
  - Each company's data is fully isolated (separate database schema)
  - Users select their company on login; the app switches context accordingly

SECURITY
  - Login via Active Directory (Windows domain credentials)
  - All API calls are authenticated with a JWT token
  - BC webhooks are authenticated with a shared secret key
  - Role-based access: admin users can create other users


5. DATA THAT COMES FROM BUSINESS CENTRAL
-----------------------------------------

With every Sales Order:
  Header — Order No., Customer No./Name, Salesperson, Route (Location),
           Sector (Posting Group), Ship-to Name/Code, Payment Terms,
           External Doc No., Quote No., Order Date, Shipment Date,
           Printed By (BC User ID), Printing Time

  Lines  — Line No., Item No., Description, Qty (Order & Base), Unit Price,
           Amount Incl. VAT, VAT %, Qty Assigned, Qty Executed,
           Customer Spec, Barcode, UOM, Posting Group

With every Sales Invoice:
  Header — All of the above PLUS: Invoice No., Posted At, E-TIMS Invoice No.,
           KRA QR Code URL, Customer KRA PIN, Salesperson Name,
           Shipment Method, Company Name/PIN/Email/VAT Reg, No. Printed

  Lines  — All order line fields PLUS: Amount Excl. VAT, Amount Incl. VAT,
           VAT Identifier, Units per Parcel


6. WHAT IS NOT IN THIS APP
---------------------------
- Stock management or inventory levels
- Pricing or discount management
- Customer master data maintenance
- Any posting or financial entries back to BC
- Purchase orders or supplier management

All financial entries remain exclusively in Business Central.
This app is read-only with respect to BC data — it only adds confirmation
and audit information on its own side.


7. TECHNICAL SUMMARY (non-technical version)
---------------------------------------------
- Web application — runs in any modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox)
- No installation required for end users
- Hosted on a company server; accessed via internal network or VPN
- Login uses existing Windows/Active Directory credentials (no new passwords)
- Data is stored in a SQL Server database on the same server as BC
- BC communicates with the app automatically in the background
